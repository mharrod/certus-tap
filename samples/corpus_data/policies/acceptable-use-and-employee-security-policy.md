# Acceptable Use & Employee Security Policy

## Purpose
Set expectations for employee behavior when using company systems, data, or access granted to support TAP operations. Supports SOC 2 CC1/CC2 and ISO 27001 A.5.

## Acceptable Use Requirements
1. Company devices and accounts are for authorized business purposes only; limited personal use is acceptable if it does not interfere with work or violate law/policy.
2. Users must not install unapproved software, disable security agents, or bypass network controls.
3. Credentials (passwords, SSH keys, API tokens) must never be shared or stored in plain text repositories.
4. Only use approved file-sharing, messaging, and document-ingestion platforms vetted by Security.
5. Report suspected phishing, malware, or data loss immediately to security@company.

## Prohibited Activities
- Exfiltrating proprietary or customer data.
- Sending offensive/harassing content via corporate systems.
- Performing vulnerability scans or penetration tests without written authorization.
- Circumventing robots.txt when performing ingestion unless explicitly approved and legally justified.

## Monitoring & Privacy Notice
Company reserves the right to monitor, log, and review usage of systems, including OpenSearch queries and ingestion jobs, in accordance with applicable laws and consent banners.

## Enforcement
Violations may lead to disciplinary action up to termination and referral to legal authorities.
