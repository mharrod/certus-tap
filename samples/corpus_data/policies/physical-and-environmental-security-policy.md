# Physical & Environmental Security Policy

## Purpose
Protect physical facilities, equipment, and infrastructure supporting TAP services in accordance with ISO 27001 A.11 and SOC 2 CC6.

## Facility Controls
- Access to offices, labs, and data centers restricted to authorized personnel with badge + MFA where available.
- Visitors must sign in, display badges, and remain escorted. Logs retained for 12 months.
- Deliveries inspected and logged; loading docks monitored.

## Environmental Protections
- Data centers equipped with redundant power, HVAC, and fire suppression systems (FM-200 or equivalent).
- Temperature, humidity, and power monitored with alerts for thresholds.
- Equipment mounted securely with cable management to prevent accidental disconnections.

## Asset Management
- Maintain inventory of servers, network gear, removable media, and hardware security modules.
- Perform asset audits annually; report discrepancies to Security & Facilities.

## Clean Desk & Media Handling
- Confidential/Restricted documents stored in locked cabinets when unattended.
- Use secure shredding bins for disposal of physical media.
- Prohibit photography in secure areas without authorization.

## Business Continuity
- Facility incident plan includes alternate workspace, remote access procedures, and vendor contacts.

## Enforcement
Noncompliance may result in badge revocation, disciplinary action, or contract termination for vendors.
